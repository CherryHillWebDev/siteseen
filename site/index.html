Be Your Own SEO Expert

Our audit gives trade business owners a quick snapshot of their online visibility in under 30 seconds. You'll see how your Google Business Profile looks to customers right now, how your website performs on mobile, how your reviews compare to local competitors, and an honest estimate of how many calls you may be missing each month.

We started SiteSeen because we kept seeing the same problem. Great trade businesses, skilled, hardworking, and owner-operated, were losing jobs online to competitors with better Google listings and faster websites. Not because they did terrible work, but because they were invisible to search engines.

We aim to fix that by giving you the information you need, so you can take your business’ presence into your own hands.

We are a small, local team of two based in South Jersey, and we are proud of it. We built this tool for our community's tradespeople, family-run shops, owner-operators, and hardworking professionals who answer their own phones and do great work every day. You deserve to be found online, and SiteSeen helps make that happen.
